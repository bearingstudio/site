About

Bearing Studio explores the intersection of design, technology, and human experience through long-form publications and research.

We believe great design begins with empathy—understanding not just what people need, but why they need it. Our work examines the ethical dimensions of industrial design, the evolution of technology, and the stories behind the objects that shape our lives.
